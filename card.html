Bengal Tiger

When a tiger wants to be heard, you’ll know about it, gang – because their roar can be heard as far as three kilometres away

Scientific Name: Panthera tigris tigris
Average Length: 2.7 - 3.1 m
Average Lifespan: 15 years
Habitat: In parts of India, Bangladesh, Nepal, Bhutan and Burma

Tigers can reach a length of up to 3.3 metres (11 feet) and weigh as much as 300 kilograms (660 pounds).Tigers are solitary hunters, and generally search for food alone at night. They quietly stalk their prey until they are close enough to pounce – then they kill their victim with a bite to the neck or back of the head.
